We are incompleto and we invite you to Surf Club, an experimental event about surfing the web on a public setting.

Three participants will surf the web for 15 minutes each in front of a small audience using the services, themes or devices they like the most.

In this first edition we’ll see Juncal, Clara, and XXX surfing.

The first event will take place on June 20th in Madrid, at Doctor Fourquet, 27, starting at 18:30. Bring Your Own Bottle!

Surf Club is an experimental event for which we are not going to charge entrance. We do it for our love to the web and we would love to share it with others.
